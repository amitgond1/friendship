Shayari

Dosti ka rang gehra ho na ho,
Dosti ka rishta gehra hota hai,
Dosti mein faasle ho na ho,
Dosti mein pyaar gehra hota hai.

Har pal ne kaha ek pal se,
Pal bhar ke liye aap meri zindagi mein aayi,
Pal bhar ka tha aapka saath magar,
Pal bhar mein aap zindagi bhar ke liye chha gayi.

Dost kam milte hain duniya mein,
Par hamesha yaad rehte hain aap jaisi dosti mein.
